Online E-CCA

During the Covid-19 Pandemic situation, in-school CCA was suspended for a few periods of time. During these periods, our CCA began to conduct sessions online to ensure that our training is not compromised. We used Google Meet to collaborate on projects and teach content. We even managed to partake in a competition, MakeX Spark, from the comfort of our own homes.
